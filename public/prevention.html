HIV Prevention

HIV isn't supposed to be scary.

Anyone is at risk for HIV

To become infected with HIV, infected blood, semen, or vaginal secretions must enter your body.

How you can get HIV

Sex without condoms
Sharing needles
Pregnancy
Breastfeeding

How you don't get HIV

Hugging
Sharing water or food
Air
Kissing
Touching Someone
Toilet seats
Insect bites
Sneezes and coughs
Bathing
Sweat

How do you prevent it?

Take PrEP
Use Condoms
Get tested and treated for STDs
Know partner's HIV status
Use clean needles when taking drugs
Get treatment when HIV+
